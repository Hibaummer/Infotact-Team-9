# TOURISM & TRAVEL ANALYTICS
### Customer Retention & Dynamic Pricing Analysis
## 📌 Project Overview
##### This project focuses on analyzing the travel, tourism, and hospitality industry to address key business challenges such as customer cancellations (churn) and inefficient pricing strategies.

##### The objective is to leverage data analytics and predictive modeling to uncover patterns in booking behavior, understand seasonal demand, and support dynamic pricing decisions that maximize revenue. 
## 🎯 Business Problem
##### In the competitive hospitality sector, businesses face:
##### 1.High booking cancellation rates
##### 2.Lack of predictive insights for customer behavior
##### 3.Inefficient pricing strategies during demand fluctuations
##### 4.This project aims to solve these issues by analyzing historical booking data and generating data-driven insights.
## 🚀 Objectives
##### Analyze customer booking patterns and cancellation behavior
##### Identify key factors influencing customer churn
##### Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
##### Build a baseline predictive model for cancellation prediction
##### Develop a dynamic pricing analysis dashboard
##### Provide actionable recommendations for revenue optimization
